Pixelate.js demonstration

Initialized via data attributes:

<img src="http://i.imgur.com/sAtUHoB.jpg" alt="http://imgur.com/gallery/zjJAc" class="img-fluid"
     data-pixelate data-value="0.25" data-reveal_on_hover="true" data-reveal_on_click="true" />

document.getElementById('myimg').pixelate({value: 0.1, reveal_on_hover: true, reveal_on_click: true})
